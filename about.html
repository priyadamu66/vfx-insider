About Us

Welcome to Connect Life VFX, where imagination meets cutting-edge technology to redefine the art of storytelling. Headquartered in Hyderabad, India, with additional studios in the USA and Dubai, Connect Life VFX is a global leader in the visual effects industry. Our work spans high-profile films, television shows, and commercials, collaborating with top production studios and advertising agencies to create visuals that captivate audiences worldwide. At Connect Life VFX, we specialize in key areas of the VFX pipeline: rotoscoping, matchmoving, paint, and compositing. Each project is an opportunity to push the boundaries of creativity and precision, delivering scenes that are not only visually stunning but also deeply immersive.
