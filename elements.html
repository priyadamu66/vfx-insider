Our Services

Delivering Outstanding Visual Experiences Through Dedication and Creativity

Rotoscoping

Comprehensive Services - We offer end-to-end rotoscoping solutions for feature films, web / TV productions, and commercials.
Advanced Techniques - Proficient in Nuke shapes, Silhouette splines, hard mattes, motion blur mattes, and open polygons for hair strands.
Precision and Realism - Expertise in lifelike movement replication, complex tracing, and clean extraction.
Commitment to Quality - High levels of Quality Control (QC) and Quality Assurance (QA) ensure timely delivery, every time.
CLV Connect Life VFX – Our Rotoscoping Services Breathe Life into Your Visuals, Making Them Feel Truly Real.

Paint

Comprehensive Paint and Cleanup Solutions - We cater to all types of paint and cleanup needs for any project, ensuring quick turnaround times.
Advanced Techniques - Proficient in rig/wire removal, reflection cleaning, and dust/scratch removal.
Seamless Integration - We deliver Nuke scripts along with rendered clean plates for easy integration into your workflow.
Pixel-Perfect Results - Whether it's frame-by-frame or clean plate, we work tirelessly to ensure flawless results, pixel by pixel.
Transform Your Visuals into Breathtaking Masterpieces with our VFX Paint Services!

Matchmove

Comprehensive Tracking Solutions - Our expert tracking services include thorough camera tracking, object tracking, VFX production tracking, and matchmove.
Advanced Techniques - Proficient in high-end Maya, FBX, and proxy geometry, ensuring seamless integration with your pipeline.
Complex Problem Solvers - We can resolve and deliver the most intricate tracking challenges.
Detailed Set Models - We deliver exhaustive set models, providing a solid foundation for your VFX projects.
Our expertise in VFX tracking software and matchmove allows us to meet the camera and object tracking needs of a diverse range of clients

Compositing

Expert Green Screen Removal - Specializing in seamless green screen removal and the integration of combined passes for 2D and 3D VFX compositing.
Rapid Delivery of Complex Shots - Capable of delivering highly intricate compositing shots in minimal timeframes.
Unmatched Quality Control - Our rigorous Quality Control (QC) and Quality Assurance (QA) processes ensure that our compositing deliveries are second to none.
Extensive Experience - Our team brings years of experience from a dedicated VFX compositing studio, ensuring top-notch service and results.
Achieve “Pixel-Perfect” Solutions with Our VFX Compositing Expertise!
