Join Our Team at Connect Life VFX

At Connect Life VFX, we are shaping the future of visual effects across the globe. As one of the fastest-growing VFX studios with operations in the USA, India, and Dubai, we pride ourselves on delivering world-class VFX solutions to some of the biggest names in entertainment and advertising.
Our journey to become an industry leader has only been possible due to the talent and dedication of our creative and technical teams. If you're passionate about storytelling, innovative VFX techniques, and cutting-edge technology, this is the place for you.

How to Apply

We are always on the lookout for talented artists, technicians, and coordinators who share our passion for VFX. If you're ready to take your career to the next level and work in a dynamic, fast-paced environment, we’d love to hear from you! Please send your resume and portfolio to

careers@connectlifevfx.com

Why Work with Us?

Global Impact

Be part of an international studio working on high-profile projects for top-tier clients in the film, television, and advertising industries.

Creative Freedom

At Connect Life VFX, we encourage innovation. Our artists and technicians are given the freedom to push the boundaries of visual storytelling.

Cutting-Edge Technology:

We invest in the latest VFX tools, software, and infrastructure to ensure our teams can perform at their best. From virtual production to photorealistic CGI, you’ll have the tools you need to bring your creative vision to life.

Diverse Culture:

With teams spread across multiple continents, we embrace diversity and are committed to building an inclusive workplace where creativity thrives.

Learning & Development:

We believe in continuous learning and provide opportunities for professional growth. Our mentorship programs, workshops, and industry partnerships help our artists and technicians stay at the forefront of VFX trends.
